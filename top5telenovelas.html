Top Five Telenovelas

What to watch

These are the top five telenovela series to watch on Netflix according to popular vote.

Rebelde

Elite Way a private school in Mexico is where a group of 6 students formed a band and became famous despite all of their drama .

La Reina del Sur

Teresa falls in love with a druglord, when he is arrested she has to escape Mexico. As she escapes she becomes a druglord herself.

Lo Que La Vida Me Robo

Monserrat falls in love with a poor man but her mother forces her to marry a rich man to solve their money problems this leads to family drama.

Teresa

A young humble girl who chooses money over love.

Rubi

A poor woman who only cares about money, she aspires to be rich by associating with rich people and in the process falls in love with a doctor.
