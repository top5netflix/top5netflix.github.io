These are the top five crime series to watch on Netflix according to popular vote.

Getting Away with Murder

A law professor who teaches how to get away with murder and other students assist in solving murder cases.

You

Joe is a stalker who falls in love easily and literally lives by "if I can't have you, no one else can.".

Criminal Minds

A group of special FBI's agent who analyze behavior to help solve on going investigations.

Narcos

Narcos features the increase of cocaine trade in Colombia. This series also describes law enforcement actions.

Breaking Bad

A high school chem teacher builds a meth lab to get money in order to take care of his family.
